# Code of Conduct

## Our Goal

VitaDash is an open source project built around collaboration, curiosity, and respect.

Everyone is welcome to participate regardless of experience level.

Whether you're submitting your first pull request or you've contributed to dozens of open source projects, we want this to be a friendly and welcoming community.

## Expected Behavior

Please:

* Be respectful.
* Be patient.
* Be constructive.
* Be welcoming.
* Assume good intentions.
* Help others learn.

Healthy technical discussions are encouraged.

Personal attacks are not.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Trolling.
* Intentionally disruptive behavior.
* Publishing private information.
* Any behavior that makes others feel unwelcome.

## Disagreements

Technical disagreements are normal.

When they happen:

* Focus on ideas, not people.
* Explain your reasoning.
* Be open to feedback.
* Respect maintainers' decisions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from the project.

## A Friendly Community

VitaDash exists because people continue to enjoy developing for and using legacy hardware.

Let's build something useful together while keeping the community welcoming, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone.
